[h1]MoveM8 - NPC Slapper[/h1]

Tired of NPCs blocking your way? Standing in doorways? Getting in your face while you're trying to build?

[b]MOVE, M8![/b]

Inspired by Preach's legendary frustration with NPCs who just won't get out of the way, this mod adds the [b]MoveM8[/b] - a satisfying tool that lets you slap those pesky Town NPCs right out of your path!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] Craft the MoveM8 weapon
[*] Hover over any NPC
[*] Give them a good slap and watch them fly!
[*] No damage dealt - just pure, satisfying knockback
[*] Complete with "SLAP!" combat text
[/list]

[h2]Crafting Recipe[/h2]
[list]
[*] 10 Silk
[*] 5 Iron Bars
[*] Crafted at a Work Bench
[/list]

[h2]How to Use[/h2]
Equip the MoveM8, hover your cursor over an NPC, and swing away. They'll get the message.

[i]"Move it, mate!"[/i]
