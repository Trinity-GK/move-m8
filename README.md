# MOVE M8

A tModLoader mod inspired by Preach Gaming's legendary frustration with NPCs blocking doorways.

## Description

NPCs blocking your doorway? Guide standing on your chest? Nurse won't get out of your face?

Craft the **Move M8** and poke those pesky NPCs out of your way!

## Features

- Jousting lance-style weapon that thrusts towards your cursor
- Knocks NPCs away without dealing damage
- Satisfying "MOVE M8!" combat text
- Early game crafting recipe

## Installation

### From Workshop (Recommended)
1. Open Terraria with tModLoader
2. Go to **Workshop** > **Download Mods**
3. Search for "MOVE M8"
4. Click **Download**
5. Enable the mod and reload

### Manual Installation
1. Download the `.tmod` file from releases
2. Place it in: `Documents/My Games/Terraria/tModLoader/Mods/`
3. Launch Terraria with tModLoader
4. Go to **Workshop** > **Manage Mods**
5. Enable "MOVE M8" and reload

### Building from Source
1. Clone/download this repository
2. Place the `NpcSlapper` folder in: `Documents/My Games/Terraria/tModLoader/ModSources/`
3. Launch Terraria with tModLoader
4. Go to **Workshop** > **Develop Mods**
5. Find "MOVE M8" and click **Build + Reload**

## Crafting Recipe

**Move M8**
- 10 Silk
- 5 Iron Bars
- Crafted at: Work Bench

## Usage

1. Craft the Move M8 at a Work Bench
2. Equip it in your hotbar
3. Point your cursor at an NPC
4. Click to thrust and knock them away!
